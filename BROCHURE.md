# Universal GxP Facility Planner

## THE PROBLEM

Building a GxP-compliant facility is a nightmare:

- **Regulations are scattered** — FDA 21 CFR 210/211, EMA Annex 1, ICH Q9, ISO 13485, GAMP 5, data integrity (ALCOA+). No single source of truth.
- **Validation is chaotic** — URS → FRS → DS → IQ → OQ → PQ → VSR. Each step has dependencies. One missed detail = months of rework.
- **Document control is broken** — SOPs, test plans, protocols, reports. Who owns what version? What's approved? What's missing?
- **Risk management is manual** — Excel spreadsheets. No traceability. No automation.
- **Change control is reactive** — Changes slip through. Scope creep. No impact assessment.
- **Readiness is guesswork** — Is the facility ready to release? No one knows until inspection day.
- **Compliance consultants drive the bus** — You have a window into 20% of the work. The rest is black box.
- **Cost overruns are standard** — 6+ months. $500K–$2M+ per facility. Delays = lost revenue.

**The Result:** Facility buildouts fail. Inspections find gaps. Timelines slip. Millions burn.
