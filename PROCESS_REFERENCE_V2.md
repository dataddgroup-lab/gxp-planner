## PHASE 1: SITE SELECTION AND PREPARATION

### Step 1: Define Site Requirements
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Define/document site criteria (proximity to suppliers, utilities, transportation). Ensure all sites meet initial criteria.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Review/document compliance with 21 CFR 210/211, 212, FDA, EU GMP, ISO 14644. Ensure all legal/regulatory requirements met.
- **EHS Specialist:** Review/document compliance with OSHA 1910, ANSI Z129.1, local zoning, environmental regulations.
- **QA Lead:** Define/document GMP requirements. Input on site requirements per QA standards.

### Step 2: Identify Potential Sites
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Compile site list. Verify all meet initial criteria.
- **Real Estate Specialist:** Market research, identify sites, initial evaluations.
- **QA Lead:** Confirm sites meet GMP standards. QA input.

### Step 3: Evaluate Potential Sites
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Lead evaluation. Document criteria.
- **Real Estate Specialist:** Site visits. Site-specific data.
- **EHS Specialist:** EHS assessments. Document hazards/concerns.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Review/document local zoning, OSHA, ANSI, environmental regs. Address all legal/regulatory aspects.
- **QA Lead:** Infrastructure review and GMP compliance. QA input.

### Step 4: Shortlist Sites
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Compile shortlist. Ensure all meet criteria.
- **Real Estate Specialist:** Confirm viability. Site-specific details.
- **EHS Specialist:** Verify EHS compliance. Document issues.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Confirm legal/regulatory compliance. Document issues.
- **QA Lead:** Verify GMP compliance. Document issues.

### Step 5: Finalize Site Selection
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Lead final decision. Ensure all parties involved.
- **Real Estate Specialist:** Confirm final site. Negotiation support.
- **EHS Specialist:** Confirm all EHS requirements met. Site-specific EHS concerns.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Confirm all legal/regulatory requirements met.
- **QA Lead:** Confirm site meets GMP. Final QA recommendations.

## PHASE 2: SITE PREPARATION

### Step 1: Obtain Necessary Permits
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Lead permit/approval process. Define required permits.
- **EHS Specialist:** Identify/document EHS permits (OSHA, ANSI, local). Obtain all.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Identify/document legal/regulatory permits (21 CFR 210/211, FDA, EU GMP). Obtain all.
- **Real Estate Specialist:** Confirm availability and compliance of permits.
- **QA Lead:** Ensure GMP compliance in permit process.

### Step 2: Site Layout and Design
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Oversee layout and design.
- **Architect/Engineer:** Design site layout and facilities.
- **EHS Specialist:** Ensure design meets EHS standards.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Ensure design meets legal/regulatory requirements.

### Step 3: Construction and Compliance
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Oversee construction.
- **Construction Manager:** Ensure construction meets design and regulatory requirements.
- **EHS Specialist:** Monitor construction for safety and compliance.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Ensure all construction complies with legal/regulatory requirements.

### Step 4: Final Inspection and Certification
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Coordinate final inspections and certifications.
- **EHS Specialist:** Conduct final EHS inspections.
- **Legal/Regulatory Specialist:** Ensure all legal/regulatory inspections complete.
- **QA Lead:** Ensure all GMP requirements met during final inspection.

---

## PHASE 3: FACILITY AND EQUIPMENT INSTALLATION

### Step 1: Equipment Procurement
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Oversee procurement.
- **Supplier Management Team:** Identify and select equipment suppliers.
- **QA Lead:** Ensure equipment meets GMP requirements.

### Step 2: Installation and Calibration
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Oversee installation and calibration.
- **Installation Team:** Install equipment.
- **Calibration Team:** Calibrate to ensure accuracy.
- **QA Lead:** Ensure all equipment meets GMP during calibration.

### Step 3: Validation and Documentation
**Roles & Actions:**
- **Project Manager:** Oversee validation and documentation.
- **Validation Team:** Perform equipment and facility validation.
- **QA Lead:** Ensure all validation documentation meets GMP.

## THE DEPENDENCY MESH — HOW EVERYTHING CONNECTS

This is the core intelligence of the app. Every node below has dependencies. A change in any node ripples through all connected nodes.

```
FACILITY
│
├── SITE SELECTION
│   ├── Criteria [PM, QA, Legal, EHS] → unlocks Potential Sites
│   ├── Evaluation [PM, RE, EHS, Legal, QA] → unlocks Shortlist
│   └── Final Decision [all roles sign off] → unlocks PERMITS
│
├── PERMITS
│   ├── EHS Permits [EHS] → dependency for: Construction Start
│   ├── Legal/Regulatory Permits [Legal] → dependency for: FDA Submission
│   └── Local Permits [RE] → dependency for: Construction Start
│
├── DESIGN AND CONSTRUCTION
│   ├── Layout/Design [PM, A/E, EHS, Legal] → dependency for: Equipment Procurement
│   ├── Construction [PM, CM, EHS, Legal] → dependency for: Equipment Installation
│   └── Final Inspection [PM, EHS, Legal, QA] → dependency for: Equipment IQ
│
├── EQUIPMENT
│   ├── Procurement [PM, Supplier, QA] → dependency for: Installation
│   ├── Installation (IQ) [Install Team, QA] → dependency for: Calibration
│   ├── Calibration (OQ) [Cal Team, QA] → dependency for: Validation
│   ├── Validation (PQ) [Val Team, QA] → dependency for: Training
│   └── Training [Train Team, QA] → dependency for: Operational Use
│
├── DOCUMENTATION
│   ├── SOPs [Doc Team, QA] → dependency for: every step that references them
│   ├── Validation Records [Val Team, QA] → dependency for: step close
│   └── Training Records [Train Team, QA] → dependency for: personnel authorization
│
├── FDA SUBMISSION
│   ├── Submission Plan [PM, QA, EHS, Legal, RE] → dependency for: Compile Docs
│   ├── Document Compilation [all roles] → dependency for: Submit
│   ├── Submit → dependency for: FDA Review
│   ├── RFI Response → dependency for: FDA Approval
│   └── FDA Approval → dependency for: Initial Operations
│
└── OPERATIONS
    ├── Operational Readiness [PM, Ops, QA] → dependency for: Initial Production
    ├── Initial Production [PM, Ops, QA] → dependency for: Ongoing Ops
    └── Ongoing Maintenance [PM, Maint, EHS, QA] → continuous
```

### Cross-Cutting Dependencies (the mesh)
- **Any design change** → triggers Change Control → re-evaluates all downstream validation
- **Any equipment change** → triggers new IQ/OQ/PQ → re-evaluates training records
- **Any SOP change** → triggers re-training for all authorized users → updates training records
- **Any permit change** → triggers Legal/Regulatory review → may block construction or operations
- **FDA RFI** → blocks all phases from closing → all roles must respond within defined SLA
- **QA hold** → blocks the step it's raised on + all downstream steps
- **CAPA opened** → linked to the step that generated it → must close before phase closes

## AI ROLE ASSISTANTS — WHAT EACH KNOWS

### PM Assistant
- Current phase and step for this facility
- Open actions across ALL roles (not just PM)
- Blockers and who owns them
- Timeline vs. actual progress
- Risk: which steps are on the critical path

### QA Assistant
- All GMP requirements for current step
- Outstanding validation records needed
- Training records that need updating
- Open CAPAs and their status
- Document review queue

### Regulatory Assistant
- Which regulations apply to current step (auto-surfaced)
- Permit status tracker with expiry alerts
- FDA submission status
- Open RFIs and response deadlines
- Regulatory change alerts (if a reg changes, flags impacted steps)

### EHS Assistant
- EHS permits status and renewal dates
- Hazard assessments due
- OSHA compliance checklist for current phase
- Incident log
- Safety inspection schedule

### Validation Assistant
- IQ/OQ/PQ status per equipment item
- Method validation queue
- Deviation log (any out-of-spec results)
- Calibration schedules and due dates
- Validation Summary Reports due

### Documentation Assistant
- Documents required for current step (auto-generated list)
- Document review and approval queue
- SOP version control (who's trained on which version)
- Document expiry alerts
- Controlled copy distribution log
